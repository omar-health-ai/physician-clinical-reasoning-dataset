## Vision

Clinical reasoning is one of the most valuable skills in medicineвЂ”and one of the most difficult capabilities to teach artificial intelligence.

This project aims to build a physician-designed, high-quality collection of structured clinical reasoning cases that demonstrate how experienced clinicians evaluate patients, prioritize differential diagnoses, apply evidence-based medicine, and make safe clinical decisions.

Rather than focusing only on the final diagnosis, each case emphasizes the reasoning process behind every clinical decision.

---

# Why This Project?

Healthcare AI models are increasingly being used to assist clinicians, educate healthcare professionals, and improve access to medical knowledge.

However, high-quality physician-generated reasoning datasets remain limited.

This repository was created to help bridge that gap by providing structured, transparent, and evidence-based clinical reasoning examples.

# Standard Case Structure

Every clinical case follows the same physician-designed framework.

- Chief Complaint
- History of Present Illness
- Past Medical History
- Medication History
- Physical Examination
- Initial Assessment
- Differential Diagnosis
- Clinical Reasoning
- Red Flags
- Recommended Investigations
- Evidence-Based Management
- International Guideline References
- Gold Standard Physician Answer
- Common AI Errors
- AI Evaluation Rubric
- Learning Points
